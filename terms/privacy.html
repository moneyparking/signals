Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy describes how information is handled in connection with the Independent Growth Signal Assessment.

Information submitted during intake may include business, operational, or contact details necessary to conduct the Assessment. No personal data unrelated to the Assessment is intentionally collected.

All information is used solely for the purpose of performing the Assessment under a fixed analytical scope. Data is not sold, licensed, or shared with third parties except where required to process payments through a third-party Merchant of Record.

Payment processing, invoicing, and applicable taxes are handled by the Merchant of Record in accordance with their own privacy and data handling policies.

Assessment-related information is retained only for as long as necessary to complete the Assessment, maintain internal records, and satisfy legal or accounting requirements.

No information provided during intake is used for marketing, advertising, or unsolicited communications.

By submitting information for the Assessment, you acknowledge and agree to the data handling practices described in this Privacy Policy.
