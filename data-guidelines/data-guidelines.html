INDEPENDENT SIGNAL ASSESSMENT
Overview Eligibility screening

Data guidelines

These guidelines apply only after eligibility confirmation. They describe the minimal inputs required to conduct an Independent Growth Signal Assessment.

The assessment is performed using aggregated performance signals only. No system access, credentials, permissions, or live integrations are required or accepted.

Required inputs (aggregated only)

Please provide data sufficient to evaluate whether the signal used to justify scaling remains economically predictive at current scale.

  • Paid acquisition spend — aggregated by period (daily, weekly, or monthly)
  • Primary scaling signal values — as used internally to trigger scaling decisions
  • Revenue outcomes — aggregated by corresponding period
  • Revenue realization lag — if known or observable

Accepted formats

  • CSV or spreadsheet exports
  • Read-only Google Sheets
  • CRM summary exports
  • Secure file transfer links
There is no required template. Data completeness affects confidence level, not eligibility or scope of the assessment.

Explicit exclusions

  • No customer-level or personally identifiable data
  • No campaign optimization details or creative assets
  • No forecasts, plans, or scenario modeling
  • No dashboards, system access, or API connections

Delivery & timing

Upon receipt of the required inputs, the Independent Growth Signal Assessment is completed within 48–72 hours, subject to capacity constraints.

The output is a single formal written determination delivered as a board-ready PDF. No recommendations, remediation steps, or follow-up discussions are included.

If the provided inputs do not allow a reliable determination of signal predictiveness, this limitation will be explicitly stated in the determination.

Independent expert determination.
Fixed scope. One-time. No advisory.
