Data submission guidelines

This page explains what data to share after eligibility confirmation. There is no required template and no system access is requested.

We work only with read-only data. No credentials, permissions, or live system access are required.

What to provide

Please share any data that reflects paid acquisition and downstream revenue. Partial data is acceptable.

  • Paid acquisition — Google Ads, LinkedIn Ads exports (last 60–90 days)
  • Activation or funnel events — trials, demos, signups, MQLs with dates
  • Revenue events — closed deals, amounts, close dates
  • Linking information — lead ID, email, or time-based attribution (if available)

Accepted formats

  • CSV or XLS exports
  • Google Sheets (view-only access)
  • CRM exports
  • Secure file links
There is no preferred format. Data quality affects confidence level, not eligibility.

What not to send

  • Campaign optimization details
  • Creative assets
  • Forecasts or plans
  • Interpretations or commentary

Delivery & timing

Once data is received, the independent economic review is completed within 48–72 hours.

The output is a single board-ready PDF report. No recommendations or follow-up discussion are included.

If the submitted data does not allow causal linkage between paid acquisition and revenue, this limitation will be explicitly stated in the report.
