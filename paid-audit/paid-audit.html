3. Basis for Determination

The determination evaluates whether the reviewed signal continues to function as a reliable proxy for downstream revenue outcomes at the current level of spend and scale.

In forming this determination, the assessment considers:

  • Consistency of the signal–revenue relationship across relevant time windows
  • Presence and persistence of material lag effects
  • Stability of realized revenue relative to incremental scaling actions
Assessment condition:

If the scaling signal remains stable
while revenue realization materially lags beyond
acceptable system thresholds,
the signal is no longer economically predictive.
