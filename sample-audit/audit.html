Independent Growth Signal Assessment — Sample

Illustrative determination · Anonymized · Public example only

1. Scope of Assessment

This document illustrates the format and substance of an independent assessment determining whether a growth signal used for scaling remains economically predictive of revenue outcomes.

This is an illustrative sample only. A live assessment is conducted as a fixed-scope, one-time engagement based on aggregated performance signals.

2. Signal Assessed

Scaling decision signal under assessment:

  • Cost Per Lead (CPL)

In this illustrative scenario, scaling decisions continued to rely on CPL stability despite a material lag and weakening correlation with realized revenue.

3. Basis for Determination (excerpt)

Assessment condition: If the scaling signal remains stable while revenue realization materially lags beyond acceptable system thresholds, the signal is no longer economically predictive.

The assessment evaluates whether the signal continues to function as a reliable proxy for downstream revenue outcomes at scale.

4. Determination

Determination: SIGNAL NO LONGER PREDICTIVE

Based on the assessed conditions, the signal used to justify scaling does not remain economically predictive at current scale.

Continued reliance on this signal materially increases the risk of inefficient capital allocation.

5. Limitations & Exclusions

  • This assessment does not include optimization or remediation guidance
  • No alternative signals are proposed
  • No implementation or operational advice is provided
  • Illustrative example only; not a live assessment
