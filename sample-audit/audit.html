Economic Signal Audit — Sample

Independent review · Anonymized · Illustrative example only

1. Audit Scope

This document illustrates an example of an independent economic audit focused on signal integrity within a paid acquisition system.

This sample is anonymized and does not represent a specific company. Values and structures are provided for demonstration of method only.

2. Signal Under Review

Primary scaling trigger evaluated in this audit:

  • Cost Per Lead (CPL)

The organization scaled paid acquisition based on CPL stability while downstream revenue showed delayed response.

3. Rule Evaluation (excerpt)

Rule ID: S-04 IF scaling trigger = CPL AND revenue lag > 2 sales cycles THEN signal validity = broken

This rule evaluates whether a proxy signal used for scaling maintains causal correlation with realized revenue.

4. Economic Impact Calculation

Marginal CAC (scaled): $1,420 Marginal CAC (baseline): $980 Delta per deal: $440 Annualized volume: 310 deals Estimated loss range: $110,000 – $160,000

Loss is calculated from marginal CAC delta multiplied by scaled deal volume beyond signal validity threshold.

5. Verdict

Signal integrity: FAILED
Scaling decision based on CPL is no longer economically valid.

6. Limitations

  • This sample does not include remediation steps
  • Values are illustrative and anonymized
  • Full rule set is not publicly disclosed
