1. Scope of Assessment

The Service Provider shall perform a one-time, fixed-scope
independent growth signal assessment (the “Assessment”).

The Assessment is strictly limited to determining whether
the signal currently used by the Client to justify scaling
paid acquisition remains economically predictive of
revenue outcomes at present scale.

No consulting, advisory, optimization, implementation,
or execution services are included.

---

2. Nature of the Assessment

The Assessment constitutes an independent expert
determination.

It does not represent consulting services, advisory
services, financial advice, investment advice, or
operational guidance.

The Assessment does not include recommendations,
alternative signals, remediation strategies, or
implementation guidance.

No outcomes, growth results, or performance improvements
are guaranteed.

---

3. Deliverable

The sole deliverable under this Agreement is a formal
written determination provided in static PDF format.

The determination states whether the assessed growth
signal is:

– economically valid at current scale, or
– no longer economically predictive at current scale.

No revisions, follow-up discussions, interpretations,
or additional services are included.
