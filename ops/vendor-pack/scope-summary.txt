Scope Summary
Scope of Independent Growth Signal Assessment:

– One-time engagement
– Fixed analytical scope
– Independent determination only
– No recommendations or optimization
– No alternative signal proposals
– No implementation guidance

Deliverable:
– Formal written determination (PDF)

The determination states whether the signal currently used
to justify scaling remains economically predictive at the
current level of spend and revenue.

The scope explicitly excludes consulting, audits, or
advisory services.
