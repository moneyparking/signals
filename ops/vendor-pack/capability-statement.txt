Capability Statement

Service Overview
We provide independent economic assessments
focused on validating the integrity of paid acquisition
scaling signals in B2B environments.

Our work determines whether the metrics used to scale
marketing spend maintain causal correlation with
realized revenue at current scale.

What We Do
- Evaluate paid acquisition signal validity
- Identify causal breaks across funnel stages
- Quantify economic risk of continued scaling
- Deliver formal, evidence-based economic verdicts

What We Do Not Do
- Campaign optimization
- Channel recommendations
- Growth strategy
- Advisory or consulting services
- Implementation or execution

Methodology
- Deterministic rule-based analysis
- Predefined economic thresholds
- No machine learning
- No opinion-based conclusions

Deliverables
- One-time written report (PDF)
- Formal economic verdict
- Confidence level and loss estimation
- No follow-up or revisions

Engagement Characteristics
- Fixed scope
- Fixed fee
- No calls required
- No ongoing relationship implied

Use Cases
- Pre-scale validation
- Board-level decision support
- Capital allocation risk assessment
