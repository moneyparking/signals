VENDOR OVERVIEW
Independent Growth Signal Assessment is an independent,
fixed-scope analytical determination service.

The service evaluates whether the signal currently used to
justify scaling paid acquisition remains economically
predictive at present scale.

The service does not provide:
– consulting,
– optimization,
– implementation,
– ongoing advisory services.

Each engagement results in a formal written determination
based solely on the data provided during intake.

The service operates independently and does not participate
in execution or decision-making.
