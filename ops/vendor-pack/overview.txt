VENDOR OVERVIEW
Independent Growth Signal Assessment


Service:
Independent Growth Signal Assessment

Nature:
One-time, fixed-scope independent expert determination.

Purpose:
To determine whether the signal used to justify scaling paid
acquisition remains economically predictive at current scale.

Deliverable:
Formal written determination (PDF).

Scope exclusions:
– No consulting or advisory services
– No optimization or remediation
– No implementation guidance
– No ongoing support

Data handling:
– Aggregated performance signals only
– No customer-level or personally identifiable data processed

Commercials:
– Fixed fee
– One-time engagement
– No subscription or renewal

This service is designed for internal decision support and
evaluation of scaling assumptions.
